About Pokepedia

Revisit your favorite pokemon memories and adventures. Learn about all of your favorite pokemon's strengths, weaknesses, and stories. Listen to your favorite soundtracks from each region. Create your dream pokemon team with our teambuilder.

Pokemon Lookup

With our pokedex containing every pokemon from your selected region, you can find information on any pokemon. Learn about each pokemon's characteristics, stats, moveset, and evolution chain with ease.

Team Generation

Automatically generate a team of six pokemon with one click, or choose your favorites and let the generator do the rest. Customize your team with options such as banning legendary pokemon, duplicate pokemon types, and more.
